2. Pig Dice Game    15/10/2020

Greetings everyone! I am pleased to present to you Pig Dice Game, a web game created using basic JavaScript as part of the Udemy course, "The Complete JavaScript Course 2020: Build Real Projects!" I must extend my sincerest gratitude to Jonas for providing such an enriching learning experience.

Now, let's delve into the rules of the game.

Each player will take turns rolling a die, with the goal of accumulating points.

  • If a player rolls a 1, their turn ends and they do not earn any points.
  • If the player rolls any other number, it is added to their turn total and their turn continues.
  • If the player decides to "hold," their turn total is added to their score and play passes to the next player.

The first player to reach a score of 100 or more points is declared the winner.
