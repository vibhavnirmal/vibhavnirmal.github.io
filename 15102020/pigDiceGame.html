Pig Dice Game    15/10/2020

Hello everyone. Welcome to Pig Dice Game. I have created this web game from basic javascript. This game was part of Udemy Course The Complete JavaScript Course 2020: Build Real Projects! Thank you so much Jonas for this amazing course.

Basic rules of this game are:

Each turn, a player repeatedly rolls a die until either a 1 is rolled or the player decides to "hold":

  • If the player rolls a 1, they score nothing and it becomes the next player's turn.
  • If the player rolls any other number, it is added to their turn total and the player's turn continues.
  • If a player chooses to "hold", their turn total is added to their score, and it becomes the next player's turn.

The first player to score 100 or more points wins.
