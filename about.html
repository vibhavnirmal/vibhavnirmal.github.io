       I am from Gujarat.  I am now pursuing my bachelors of computer science from LD College of Engineering, Ahmedabad. I am making projects based on python, computer vision and machine learning.


       I have created my blog site where I will be uploading my projects on which I have been working on in Python Language. You can see my projects on Github Account as well as here in my blogs section.


       I want to share one thing with everyone that you should not learn to code/program by learning from basics. "Just take one project or idea in which you are interested(no matter how small it is), Google how do I get started with this idea and learn to use Stack Overflow". It can be just a small script to detect your face, it can be simple script to write words into csv file, it can be script to bifurcate photos captured in one camera or another, it can be anything. You will see, in no time you will be able understand all the basic things related to your idea. Sometimes you will also end up learning some advanced or we can say pro things too. You can excel in just few weeks.


       This is how I learn new things. These are my personal thoughts, there are plenty of tutorials(ie youtube) and online reading materials that you can watch, refer and read. There are some pretty amazing sites too, which lets you interact with other developers.
