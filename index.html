About Me

My name is Vibhav Nirmal and I am a graduate student in computer science at the University of Texas at Arlington. I received my bachelor's degree in computer engineering from LD College of Engineering in Ahmedabad and have over one year of professional experience in software development.

I am interested in the field of unmanned vehicle systems and their various applications, particularly motion planning, control, and perception. I also enjoy using Arduino and Raspberry Pi to create IoT solutions through hands-on experimentation. My participation in the University of Texas at Arlington's AutonoBots club allowed me to work on robot localization and sensor fusion with lidar data during the National Robotics Challenge in April 2022.

With a strong background in mathematics, programming logic, and cross-platform coding, I am eager to use my skills to develop innovative solutions in robotics, artificial intelligence, and computer vision. I believe in the importance of collaboration, effective communication, and creativity in all my work.

Thank you for stopping by and taking an interest in my work. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to reach out to me.
